Dr. Tibor Timar is an excellent diagnostician and unless really necessary hardly ever uses invasive tests (like blood test etc) with young children. He's seen our ill son and daughter many times and was never wrong with his diagnosis and prognosis on the illness. Every time it did exactly what he said it would do.

He doesn't like prescribing medicine, unless he sees it as inevitable. Only once in those years were we prescribed antibiotics and I'm thankful for that. Our toddler's immunity seems to be much stronger, also thanks to allowing him deal with simple illnesses on his own and build up his immune system in this crucial period. He has an extremely calming effect, as he explains how illness behaves and assures you what's normal. He's also seen me during my pregnancy whenever I was ill, or was able to provide an advice via telephone. Our whole family went to him.

He's able to answer your calls out of the usual office hours and even come to

you on weekends or national holidays, provided he's in Budapest. Once he saved us an unnecessary trip to a hospital when he came to see our son, following a serious looking head injury, on a national holiday. After checking him properly and explaining some precautionary steps for the next 24 hours, he was again, as always, right about his diagnosis of the injury.

He works independently and is not bribed by pharmaceutical companies, which also influences his stance on vaccinations. He will tell you honestly his opinion about each vaccination, explain the pros and cons and certainly will not push any vaccination on you. Some vaccinations he recommends, some he strongly disagrees with and the rest he's neutral about. The decision is always yours without any judgement.

Most international insurance policies cover his costs and he takes care of all the paperwork for you. All he needs is your signature. He does regular house visits, which is a godsend, if more family members are ill.. or the mother herself with a small child. He's very reliable and always on time.

I only wish we could find a family doctor as good as he was everywhere we go.
